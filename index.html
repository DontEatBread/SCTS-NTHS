About Our Chapter

The Sumter Career & Technology Center chapter of the National Technical Honor Society promotes excellence in career and technical education. We recognize students who demonstrate outstanding skill, leadership, and character across all program areas. We run events, support members with scholarships and opportunities, and partner with local employers.
